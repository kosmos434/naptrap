Rules
  1. Place humans on the left, vampires on the right
  2. Roll the dice for team movement
  3. Players move during appropriate time (day for humans, night for vampires)
  4. Move exactly the rolled amount or until you land on an enemy
  5. Lay down traps...or not
  6. Moving over or landing on a trap traps you for 1 turn
  7. Traps are removed after trapping 1 or more players for 1 turn
  8. Land on the enemy to defeat them ⚰
